If you’re using a Wi-Fi adapter on your device to connect to the internet, you’ll need to make sure this is working. This could be an adapter on your Honor V Purse itself, especially if you’re using an older device, or if you’re using an adapter to boost the ranger of your router network.

## Part 6. Check SSID and IP address on Android

To make a Wi-Fi connection work, your Honor V Purse needs to match two codes that connect and relate to your router to establish the connection and work correctly. These are known as the SSID and the IP address.

Every wireless device will have its own codes and making sure they’re matched to the network you’re connecting to is crucial to ensuring the internet is working on your Honor V Purse. Here’s how to check on your Honor V Purse to make sure everything is correct.

  1. Tap the Settings menu option, followed by Wi-Fi on your Honor V Purse
  2. Turn on your Wi-Fi network and connect it to your router

  4. Once connected, tap the Wi-Fi network and you will see the IP address. Check both your phone and the router codes to make sure this number matches

When these numbers are matching, if your Android is still not connecting to wifi, you’ll know this wasn’t the problem.
